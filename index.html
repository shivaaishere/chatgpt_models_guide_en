📝 Content Creation & Copywriting

Choose: GPT-4o
Why: Optimal balance of quality, speed, and stability. Excellent at understanding briefs and creating structured content.
Alternative: GPT-4.5 for experimental creative content with more natural tone.

💻 Programming & Technical Docs

Choose: GPT-4.1
Why: 21.4% better at development tasks on SWE-bench, understands large codebases, better instruction following.
Alternative: GPT-4.1 mini for simple scripts and quick fixes.

🖼️ Image Analysis & Visual Tasks

Choose: o3 or o4-mini
Why: First models that can "think with images" — integrate visual information directly into reasoning chains.
When o3: Complex diagram analysis, detailed study
When o4-mini: Quick processing, simple image questions

⚡ Quick Questions & Chat

Choose: GPT-4.1 mini or o4-mini
Why: Maximum response speed while maintaining quality.
Alternative: Stay with GPT-4o if you prefer consistency over speed.

📊 Complex Analytics & Research

Choose: o3
Why: Designed to handle more complex tasks than existing model types like GPT-4o. Better for multi-step reasoning.
Alternative: GPT-4.1 for structured analysis with large context windows.

🧪 Experimental Tasks

Choose: GPT-4.5
Why: Better understands what humans mean and interprets subtle cues with greater nuance and "EQ", but remember — it's a testing model.
Alternative: o3 for experimental reasoning tasks with more stability.

⚡ Quick Switch Guide

Stay on GPT-4o if:

  • Working with regular tasks
  • Need stable results
  • Creating business content
  • Don't want to experiment

Switch to other models if:

  • GPT-4.1: writing or debugging code
  • o3: solving complex logical problems
  • o4-mini: need speed with images
  • GPT-4.5: experimenting with creativity
